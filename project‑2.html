Overview

A secure system for managing patient records, appointments, and clinical workflows.

The Problem

Clinics often rely on outdated systems or spreadsheets, leading to errors, delays, and poor patient experience.

The Solution

I built a modern healthcare management platform that centralizes patient data, streamlines scheduling, and improves communication between staff and patients.

Features

  • Patient profiles and medical history
  • Appointment scheduling and reminders
  • Doctor dashboard
  • Secure authentication
  • Role‑based access control

Tech Stack

  • Frontend: React, Tailwind CSS
  • Backend: Node.js, Express
  • Database: PostgreSQL
  • Security: JWT, bcrypt
